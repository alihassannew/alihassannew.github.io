About Us

Ali Hassan & Associates was founded in 2003 with a strong vision of providing exceptional legal representation in the UAE. With over 18 years of experience the firm has proven success and understanding of the legal system and its framework. This success is founded on the philosophy of offering support and professional advice to clients whilst still dealing with each case in a culturally sensitive manner.

Regardless of the area of law, Ali Hassan’s lawyers are dedicated to achieving the best possible outcome for our clients with sophisticated yet practical legal advice to avoid unnecessary complications and stress for the client. With ongoing and frequent expansion plans, the firm has always been able to cater to the growing demand while maintaining integrity and professionalism.
